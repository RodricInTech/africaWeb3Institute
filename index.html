About the Fellowship

A focused curriculum blended with mentorship and community support. You'll start with fundamentals, then move quickly into building: smart contracts, dApp frontends, tooling, and deployment.

Cohort structure

Weekly workshops, office hours, and build sprints. Small group feedback ensures you keep moving.

Mentor guidance

Mentors review your plans, help you debug, and share practical experience from real-world projects.

Capstone mindset

Each fellow ships a capstone by the end of the program, suitable for a portfolio and demo.

Requirements

We're looking for motivated builders with a learning mindset. If Web3 is new to you, that's okay as long as you can commit time and learn quickly.

You should be able to

  • Commit to weekly workshops and build sprints
  • Communicate progress (what's working, what's blocked)
  • Follow security-minded development practices

Preferred experience

  • Basic JavaScript/TypeScript or Solidity familiarity
  • Comfort with learning new tools and documentation
  • Ability to collaborate and iterate on feedback
